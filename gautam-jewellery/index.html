Our Story

Heritage of Trust & Gold

Gautam Jewellers has been a cornerstone of gold and diamond jewellery in Mira Bhayandar since 2000. Founded on the values of purity, transparency, and artistry, we have earned the trust of thousands of families who chose us for their most precious moments — weddings, anniversaries, and new beginnings.

Located at Shop No 04/A, Saraswati Krupa Sv Road, Cross Garden Rd, Navghar, our store is a destination for those who seek quality jewellery at honest prices. We carry an exquisite range of 18kt, 22kt, and 24kt gold jewellery alongside certified diamond pieces for every budget.
